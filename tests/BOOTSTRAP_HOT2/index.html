Margherita Supreme: Our best-selling pizza, the Margherita Supreme, is a symphony of flavors. Crafted with a crisp, stone-baked crust, this pizza is generously topped with fresh, vine-ripened tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, fragrant basil, and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil. Every bite bursts with the essence of traditional Italian pizza-making, delivering pure, unadulterated bliss

Fiery Buffalo Wings: Our Fiery Buffalo Wings are the ultimate crowd-pleaser. These succulent, golden-brown wings are marinated in a spicy buffalo sauce that’s equal parts tangy and fiery. Perfectly balanced with a hint of garlic and a touch of butter, they’re served with a side of cool, creamy ranch or blue cheese dressing. These wings are the ideal companion to our Margherita Supreme, making for a meal that’s simply unforgettable.

Blissful Berry Lemonade: is a refreshing blend of sweet and tart flavors. This invigorating beverage combines freshly squeezed lemons with a medley of mixed berries, creating a vibrant, thirst-quenching drink. Perfectly balanced with just the right amount of sweetness, it’s served chilled and garnished with a slice of lemon and a sprig of mint. Ideal for pairing with any of our pizzas or wings, this lemonade is the ultimate refresher to complement your meal.
