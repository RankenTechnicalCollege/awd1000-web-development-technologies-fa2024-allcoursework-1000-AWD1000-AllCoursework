Combat

Players can disarm opponents using counter-attacks while unarmed, and if they steal an enemy's weapon, they can follow up with an attack that instantly kills the enemy. The game also requires players to learn a smart offense to get around some enemies solid defenses.

Stealth

Significantly enhanced compared to the first game. Players can use various hiding spots like haystacks, benches, and wells to remain undetected. Additionally, Ezio can now grab enemies and pull them into hiding spots for quick, silent takedowns. The game also introduces new stealth features such as blending into crowds and using distractions to avoid detection.

Puzzles

Puzzles play a significant role in the gameplay experience. This includes finding and solving glyphs. Another notable puzzle involves collecting and arranging Codex pages to form a map of the world.
